How to Play CrazyCrosswords

Game Overview

CrazyCrosswords is a unique word puzzle game where you rearrange letters to form words both horizontally and vertically. Each puzzle presents you with a grid of letters that you need to reorganize to create valid words in both directions.

Basic Rules

Follow these simple rules to master CrazyCrosswords:

  • Rearrange tiles to form correct words in all rows and columns
  • Tap and drag any letter to swap it with any other letter on the board
  • Form words that match the hints:
    • Rows: Read left to right
    • Columns: Read top to bottom
  • Correct words turn green and update the hint icon
  • Complete the level by forming all correct words
Pro Tip: With the ability to swap any letters, try focusing on completing one word at a time. Look for letters you need anywhere on the board!

Controls

Intuitive controls make CrazyCrosswords easy to play:

  • Tap and hold any letter to select it
  • Drag the letter to any other position on the board to swap
  • Tap hint buttons on the sides to see target words

Powerups

When you need a little help, these powerups can save the day:

Reveal Word

This powerup reveals the correct arrangement of letters for the currently selected hint (row or column). Use it when you're stuck on a particularly tricky word!

Shuffle Hint

This powerup rearranges the letters in the currently selected row or column, giving you a fresh perspective that might help you spot the solution.

Using Powerups

Powerups require coins to use. Select a hint first (row or column), then tap the powerup you want to use. The powerup will affect only the currently selected hint.

Coins & Progression

Earn rewards as you play and progress through the game:

  • Earning Coins: You can earn coins by watching ads or purchase them from the in-app store
  • XP System: Completing levels earns you XP
  • Level Up Rewards: When you gain enough XP to level up your player profile (separate from adventure level progress), you receive coins as a reward

Frequently Asked Questions

What makes a correct word?

A valid word must match the hint shown for that row or column in the puzzle, and it must be a word found in our English dictionary. The game will highlight words in green when they match their corresponding hints, helping you track your progress toward solving the puzzle.

How do I use hints?

Tap the hint buttons on the sides of the grid. Row hints show what word you need to make horizontally, while column hints show vertical target words.

Where can I move letters?

You can swap any letter with any other letter on the board! This gives you complete freedom to arrange letters exactly how you want them.

How do I complete a level?

Form all the correct words shown in the hints for both rows and columns. When all words are correct, you'll automatically advance to the next level.

What happens when I level up?

There are two types of levels in CrazyCrosswords:

  • Adventure Levels: These are the individual puzzle levels you complete in sequence.
  • Player Level: As you complete puzzles, you earn XP that increases your overall player level. Each time you level up, you receive coins as a reward.
