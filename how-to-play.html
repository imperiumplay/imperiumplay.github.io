How to Play CrazyCrosswords

Game Overview

CrazyCrosswords is a unique word puzzle game where you rearrange letters to form words both horizontally and vertically. Each puzzle presents you with a grid of letters that you need to reorganize to create valid words in both directions.

Basic Rules

  • Rearrange tiles to form correct words in all rows and columns
  • Drag tiles horizontally or vertically to swap positions
  • Form words that match the hints:
    • Rows: Read left to right
    • Columns: Read top to bottom
  • Correct words turn green and update the hint icon
  • Valid words not matching hints count as bonus words
  • Complete the level by forming all correct words
Pro Tip: Look for bonus words! When you make additional valid words besides the required ones, they count as bonus points.

Controls

  • Tap and hold a letter to select it
  • Drag the letter to another position to swap
  • Drag backwards to undo your last swap
  • Tap hint buttons on the sides to see target words

Frequently Asked Questions

What makes a valid word?

Valid words must be at least 4 letters long and exist in our English dictionary. The game highlights valid words as you form them.

What are bonus words?

When you form additional valid words besides the required ones, they count as bonus words. These add to your score and unlock special achievements.

How do I use hints?

Tap the hint buttons on the sides of the grid. Row hints show what word you need to make horizontally, while column hints show vertical target words.

Can I undo a move?

Yes! After swapping letters, you can immediately drag backwards to undo the swap. This helps when experimenting with different word combinations.

How do I complete a level?

Form all the correct words shown in the hints for both rows and columns. When all words are correct, you'll automatically advance to the next level.
